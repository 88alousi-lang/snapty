# Snapty Photographer Platform Policies

**Effective Date:** March 11, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## INSURANCE POLICY

**Photographer Responsibility:** All photographers must maintain professional liability insurance with minimum coverage of $1,000,000.

**Coverage Requirements:**
- General liability insurance
- Professional indemnity insurance
- Equipment insurance
- Vehicle insurance (if using personal vehicle for transport)

**Hold Harmless:** Photographers agree to hold harmless HA Media LLC and Snapty from all claims, damages, and liabilities arising from:
- Injury to persons
- Damage to property
- Equipment failure
- Accidents or incidents during shoots
- Any other claims related to photography services

**Proof of Insurance:** Photographers may be required to provide proof of insurance upon request.

## DRONE FAA POLICY

**FAA Part 107 Certification Required:** All photographers offering drone services must hold a valid FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate.

**Certificate Upload Requirements:**
- Certificate must be uploaded within **30 days** of registration
- Certificate must be current and valid
- Expired certificates will automatically disable drone services
- Renewal must occur before expiration

**Automatic Service Disabling:** If a valid FAA certificate is not uploaded within 30 days:
- Drone services will be automatically disabled
- Clients will not see drone service options
- Photographer cannot accept drone bookings
- Services can be re-enabled upon certificate upload

**FAA Compliance:** Photographers must comply with all FAA regulations:
- Airspace restrictions and NOTAM requirements
- Weather and visibility requirements
- Altitude and distance limitations
- No-fly zone compliance
- Commercial operation requirements

**Liability:** Photographers assume all liability for FAA violations and any incidents involving drone operations.

## PROPERTY DAMAGE POLICY

**Full Responsibility:** Photographers are fully responsible for any property damage caused during shoots.

**Coverage Includes:**
- Accidental damage to client property
- Damage to fixtures or structures
- Damage to landscaping or outdoor features
- Theft or loss of client property
- Any other damage occurring during the shoot

**Insurance Requirement:** Photographers must maintain property damage liability coverage as part of their professional insurance.

**No Reimbursement:** HA Media LLC will not reimburse photographers for property damage claims. Photographers must resolve directly with clients or through insurance.

**Client Disputes:** Property damage disputes will be handled through:
1. Direct negotiation between photographer and client
2. Mediation through Snapty support
3. Legal action if necessary

## CANCELLATION POLICY

**Cancellation Tracking:** Snapty tracks all cancellations and no-shows.

**Photographer Cancellation Penalties:**
- **1-2 Cancellations:** Warning
- **3-5 Cancellations:** Reduced visibility in search results
- **6+ Cancellations:** Account suspension review
- **Repeated Pattern:** Permanent ban

**No-Show Definition:** Failure to appear for a scheduled booking without cancellation 24 hours in advance.

**No-Show Penalties:**
- **1 No-Show:** Warning and client refund
- **2 No-Shows:** Account suspension (7 days)
- **3+ No-Shows:** Permanent account termination

**Valid Cancellation Reasons:**
- Emergency or illness (with documentation)
- Equipment failure (with proof)
- Weather conditions (with verification)
- Client cancellation

**Invalid Cancellation Reasons:**
- Better opportunity elsewhere
- Overbooked schedule
- Changed mind about booking
- Any non-emergency reason

## DATA PROTECTION POLICY

**Client Data Confidentiality:** All client information is confidential and must be protected.

**Prohibited Actions:**
- Storing client contact information
- Sharing client data with third parties
- Using client data for marketing
- Selling or trading client information
- Accessing client data beyond what's necessary for the booking

**Data Retention:** Photographers must delete all client data within 30 days of booking completion, including:
- Client name and contact information
- Property address and details
- Photos and metadata
- Communications and messages
- Any other personal information

**Data Security:** Photographers must:
- Use secure storage methods
- Encrypt sensitive information
- Use strong passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Report any data breaches immediately

**Violations:** Data protection violations may result in:
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban
- Legal action
- Liability for damages

## AI USAGE POLICY

**Prohibited AI Training:** Snapty photos cannot be used for AI training datasets.

**Prohibited Uses:**
- Training machine learning models
- Fine-tuning AI image generators
- Creating synthetic datasets
- Any automated learning from photos

**Prohibited Commercial Uses:**
- Uploading to stock photography websites
- Selling photos on third-party platforms
- Using photos for commercial purposes without consent
- Licensing photos to other businesses

**Permitted Uses:**
- Using photos in your professional portfolio (with client consent)
- Marketing your photography services
- Creating case studies or examples
- Personal use and archival

**Watermarking:** Photos may be watermarked with photographer credit for portfolio use only.

**Violations:** AI usage violations may result in:
- Immediate account suspension
- Permanent ban
- Legal action for copyright infringement
- Liability for damages

## PAYMENT AND PAYOUT POLICY

**Commission Structure:** Snapty retains 35% commission; photographer receives 65%.

**Payout Schedule:** Payouts are processed:
- Daily to Stripe Connect account
- Weekly to bank account
- On demand (with processing fee)

**Payout Requirements:**
- Valid Stripe Connect or bank account
- Completed tax documentation (W-9)
- No outstanding disputes or chargebacks
- Account in good standing

**Holds:** Snapty may place holds on payouts for:
- Disputed transactions
- Refunds or chargebacks
- Quality issues under investigation
- Compliance violations

## ACCOUNT SUSPENSION AND TERMINATION

**Suspension Reasons:**
- Violation of photographer agreement
- Repeated quality issues
- Multiple client complaints
- Non-compliance with policies
- Cancellation or no-show patterns
- Data protection violations
- Professional conduct violations

**Suspension Process:**
1. Warning notice with specific violations
2. 7-day suspension (first offense)
3. 30-day suspension (second offense)
4. Permanent termination (third offense)

**Immediate Termination:** Account may be immediately terminated for:
- Fraudulent activity
- Criminal activity
- Serious data breaches
- Harassment or discrimination
- Repeated violations after warnings

**Appeal Process:** Photographers may appeal suspension within 14 days by:
1. Submitting written explanation
2. Providing evidence of compliance
3. Proposing corrective actions
4. Awaiting admin review
