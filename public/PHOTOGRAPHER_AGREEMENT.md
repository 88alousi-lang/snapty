You are an independent contractor, not an employee of HA Media LLC or Snapty. You are responsible for:
- All applicable taxes (federal, state, and local)
- Self-employment taxes
- Workers' compensation insurance
- Professional liability insurance
- All business expenses and equipment

- All photographs, images, and content created during bookings are owned by HA Media LLC for a period of **10 years** from the date of creation
- You grant HA Media LLC a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, distribute, and modify the photos
- You may not reuse, republish, or sell photos without written permission from HA Media LLC
- You may use photos in your portfolio only with written consent from HA Media LLC

## 3. CLIENT DATA CONFIDENTIALITY

- All client information, property details, and personal data must be kept strictly confidential
- You may not share client information with third parties
- You may not use client data for any purpose other than completing the booking
- Violation of confidentiality may result in account suspension or legal action

## 4. NO DIRECT CLIENT SOLICITATION

- You may not solicit clients outside the Snapty platform
- You may not collect client contact information for direct bookings
- You may not encourage clients to book directly with you to avoid Snapty fees
- Violation may result in immediate account suspension and legal action

## 5. QUALITY STANDARDS FOR REAL ESTATE PHOTOGRAPHY

You agree to maintain professional standards:
- High-resolution images (minimum 4K equivalent)
- Proper lighting and composition
- Accurate color representation
- Timely delivery of files
- Professional editing and post-processing
- Compliance with client requirements and special instructions

## 6. DELIVERY DEADLINES

- Standard delivery: 24-48 hours after booking
- Express delivery: 12 hours (if selected)
- Failure to meet deadlines may result in:
  - Reduced payment
  - Client refund
  - Account warnings
  - Suspension for repeated violations

## 7. PROFESSIONAL EQUIPMENT REQUIREMENTS

- Professional-grade camera (DSLR, mirrorless, or equivalent)
- Minimum 20MP resolution
- Professional lenses (wide-angle, standard, telephoto)
- Lighting equipment (flash, reflectors, or continuous lights)
- Tripod and stabilization equipment
- Backup equipment in case of failure
- Drone (if offering drone services) - must be FAA Part 107 certified
